About Quiz App

This Quiz App was developed to provide an intuitive, user-friendly platform for learners to test their knowledge and improve through immediate feedback. It addresses the need for accessible, interactive learning tools, especially for students and educators.

Unlike other platforms that are often cluttered with subscription-based or unnecessary features, this app focuses on simplicity and effectiveness. By allowing users to take quizzes and receive instant feedback, the app encourages active learning and self-assessment..
